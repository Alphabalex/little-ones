About Us

At Beckwin International School, we provide a comprehensive educational journey that nurtures and supports every child from Creche to the Grade 5.

Our holistic approach ensures that each student receives not only a strong academic foundation but also the emotional and social skills needed to succeed in today’s rapidly evolving world.

We believe that every child is unique, which is why we offer personalized learning experiences designed to bring out the best in each student. From their very first steps in Creche to the moment they step into their university life, we are committed to their growth, well-being, and success.

Our dedicated team of educators and staff work hand-in-hand with parents to create a nurturing, safe, and stimulating environment where children can thrive.

At Beckwin, we go beyond traditional teaching – we guide, inspire, and equip our students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to face the challenges of the future.

With a focus on character development, critical thinking, and global citizenship, we prepare our students to not only excel academically but also to make a positive impact in the world around them.

Class levels

Our Creche program provides a warm and nurturing environment for your little one to thrive. With a focus on sensory development and early socialization, we ensure your child feels secure, happy, and loved.

The Pre-Nursery program introduces foundational learning through play-based activities. We foster curiosity and creativity while building essential motor and communication skills.

Our Nursery program balances structured learning with free exploration. Children engage in activities that enhance cognitive skills, problem-solving, and collaboration in a joyful setting.

Kindergarten 1 builds on early learning experiences, introducing children to early literacy and numeracy. Through interactive and hands-on activities, they develop a love for learning.

In Grade K, we focus on cultivating foundational academic skills while encouraging critical thinking and independence. It's the perfect bridge to structured elementary education.

1st Grade marks the start of formal schooling. With an emphasis on reading, writing, and mathematics, children develop confidence and a deeper understanding of core concepts.

2nd Grade builds on foundational knowledge with a more advanced curriculum. We encourage curiosity and foster skills like collaboration, comprehension, and problem-solving.

By 3rd Grade, students are ready for a deeper dive into science, math, and language arts. Our program supports analytical thinking and creativity to prepare them for future challenges.

In 4th Grade, students explore topics in greater detail, focusing on projects and group activities. We promote responsibility, leadership, and a collaborative spirit.

5th Grade prepares students for the transition to middle school. With an enriched curriculum, they build on critical thinking, time management, and academic independence.
